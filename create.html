Contests

Participate every day in social contests onThe Sandbox Twitteraccount to get a chance to win a Season 2 Alpha Pass.

1,000 Season 2 Alpha Passes are distributed every Thursday.

You can participate in each daily contest only once but you can maximize your chances of winning by participating in the daily contest every day of the week!

Daily contest activities will range from trying to find Easter eggs in the game or capturing cool screenshots.

Please note that if you get a Season 2 Alpha Pass through daily contests you will no longer be elegible to receive one from the Raffle at the end of Alpha Season 2.
